The extended Kalman filter(EKF) is the nonlinear version of the Kalman filter.

# Predict

$$
q^{k-1}_k \approx \begin{bmatrix}
\dfrac {\omega_x \Delta t} {2} &
\dfrac {\omega_y \Delta t} {2} &
\dfrac {\omega_z \Delta t} {2} &
1
\end{bmatrix}^T
$$
